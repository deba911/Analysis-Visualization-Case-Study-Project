# Analysis-Visualization-Case-Study-Project-
This project involves analyzing retail sales data using SQL for data extraction and manipulation, and Excel for visualization and dashboard creation. The dataset includes customer, employee, order, and product information. <br>
<h2>About Dataset</h2>
Dataset contains 4 tables:-
1. Customer Sales <br>
2. Employee Sales <br>
3. Order Sales <br>
4. Products Sales 
<h2>Analysis using SQL</h2>
• Total Sales by Month <br>
• Top 5 Products by Revenue <br>
• Top Customers by Total Purchase <br>
• Sales by Employee <br>
• Average Order Value per Month <br>
• Most Frequently Sold Product <br>
• Customers with No Orders <br>
• Monthly Sales Growth % <br>
• Orders by Country/Region 
<h2>Visualization</h2>
• Monthly Revenue Trend Line Chart <br>
• Top 5 Products Bar Chart <br>
• Sales by Employee Pie Chart <br>
• Sales by region
